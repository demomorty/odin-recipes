Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and gooey melted mozzarella, all nestled between tender lasagna noodles. This hearty, comforting dish is baked to perfection, creating a golden, bubbly top layer. It's perfect for family dinners or special occasions and can be customized with various fillings like vegetables or alternative proteins.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • Ground beef (1 pound / 450g)
  • Ground pork or Italian sausage (1/2 pound / 225g)
  • Olive oil (2 tablespoons)
  • Onion, finely chopped (1 medium)
  • Garlic, minced (3 cloves)
  • Crushed tomatoes (28 oz / 800g can)
  • Tomato paste (6 oz / 170g can)
  • Tomato sauce (14 oz / 400g can)
  • Water (1/2 cup / 120ml)
  • Sugar (2 teaspoons)
  • Dried basil (1 teaspoon)
  • Dried oregano (1 teaspoon)
  • Salt (1/2 teaspoon)
  • Black pepper (1/4 teaspoon)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, 1/4 teaspoon)

For the Cheese Mixture:

  • Ricotta cheese (15 oz / 425g)
  • Egg (1 large)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (1/4 cup / 25g)
  • Fresh parsley (1 tablespoon, or 1 teaspoon dried)
  • Pinch of salt and black pepper

For Assembly:

  • Lasagna noodles (12, cooked or oven-ready)
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese (2 cups / 200g)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (1/4 cup / 25g)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the ground beef and pork, and cook until browned, breaking it into small pieces.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Sauté until softened and fragrant, about 3 minutes.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Add sugar, dried basil, oregano, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  4. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer uncovered for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, the egg, Parmesan cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix until well blended and set aside.
  6. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). If not using oven-ready lasagna noodles, cook them until al dente according to package instructions. Drain and lay flat to avoid sticking.
  7. Spread 1 cup of the prepared meat sauce over the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  8. Lay 3-4 lasagna noodles over the sauce, slightly overlapping them. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture on top, followed by 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese, and then 1 cup of meat sauce.
  9. Repeat the layering process (noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, and sauce) two more times, finishing with meat sauce as the top layer.
  10. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese evenly over the top.
  11. Cover the dish with aluminum foil (sprayed with non-stick spray to prevent sticking). Bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  13. Allow the lasagna to rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.
