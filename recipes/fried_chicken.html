Fried Chicken

Description

Fried chicken is a beloved comfort food known for its crispy, golden-brown crust and tender, juicy interior. This dish is made by coating pieces of chicken in a seasoned batter or breading and deep-frying them to perfection. It's a versatile recipe that can be served with a variety of side dishes, making it a popular choice for family meals, picnics, and gatherings.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • Chicken pieces (drumsticks, thighs, wings, etc.) - 2 pounds / 900g
  • Buttermilk (2 cups / 480ml)
  • Salt (1 teaspoon)
  • Black pepper (1/2 teaspoon)

For the Coating:

  • All-purpose flour (2 cups / 250g)
  • Cornstarch (1/4 cup / 30g)
  • Garlic powder (1 teaspoon)
  • Onion powder (1 teaspoon)
  • Paprika (1 teaspoon)
  • Cayenne pepper (optional, 1/4 teaspoon)
  • Baking powder (1 teaspoon)
  • Salt (1 teaspoon)
  • Black pepper (1/2 teaspoon)

For Frying:

  • Vegetable oil or peanut oil (enough for deep-frying)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chicken pieces, buttermilk, salt, and black pepper. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight for best results.
  2. In another bowl, mix the flour, cornstarch, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, baking powder, salt, and black pepper to create the coating.
  3. Remove the chicken from the buttermilk and let any excess drip off. Dredge each piece in the flour mixture, pressing firmly to ensure a thick, even coating.
  4. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large, heavy pot to 350째F (175째C). Ensure there is enough oil to fully submerge the chicken pieces.
  5. Carefully place a few pieces of chicken in the hot oil, making sure not to overcrowd the pot. Fry for 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is golden brown and fully cooked (internal temperature of 165째F / 74째C).
  6. Transfer the fried chicken to a wire rack or paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  7. Repeat with the remaining chicken pieces. Serve hot with your favorite sides like mashed potatoes, coleslaw, or biscuits.
